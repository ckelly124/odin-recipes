Lolo Rolls

Recipe transcribed from my Lolo's handwritten recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups Bread Flour
  • 2-3 Tablespoons Non-Fat Dry Milk
  • 4-5 Tablespoons Sugar
  • ½ teaspoons Salt
  • 2 tablespoons (⅓ of a bar) of “Unsalted Sweet Butter”
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 Teaspoons (1 bag) of active dry yeast
  • 1 ¼ Cup Water
  • Corn Meal (for dusting)
Bread Maker Directions:

Bread Making Machine up to “Dough Maker”. Other machines, I stop after the first rising, before the second kneading.

Principle:
  • First Kneading
  • First Rise
After first rising on Dough Mode, remove from the machine and with buttered hands (to prevent dough from sticking to fingers), mold into balls. This usually makes 16-18 round balls or rolls.

Put Roll on baking sheets (or baking pan, covered lightly with corn meal. Put Baking pan with rolls, place in warm room for 20-30 minutes. Rolls will start rising.

Bake at 350 degrees for 15-20 minute. Watch closely and remove when rolls are brown.
